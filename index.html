How Our Approach Helps

Gentle, personalized support for every stage of your journey.

Whether you're navigating fertility, preparing for pregnancy and birth, or seeking support through life's next chapter, our approach combines guided hypnosis, relaxation, education, and personalized coaching to nurture the connection between mind and body.

Every session is designed to complement your medical care, helping you feel calmer, more confident, and better equipped with practical tools to support your emotional and physical well-being.

01

Reduce Stress & Restore Balance

Stress can affect both emotional and physical well-being. Guided relaxation and hypnosis help calm the nervous system, ease anxiety, and create space for greater balance, resilience, and healing.

02

Build Confidence & Emotional Resilience

Whether you're trying to conceive, preparing for IVF, expecting your baby, or approaching birth, personalized coaching and guided hypnosis can help quiet fear, strengthen confidence, and foster a more positive mindset.

03

Strengthen the Mind-Body Connection

Visualization, relaxation, and positive suggestion help deepen awareness of the powerful connection between mind and body, encouraging trust in yourself and supporting your overall wellness throughout every stage of your journey.

Support Your Unique Journey

No two journeys are alike. Every individual and couple receives compassionate, personalized support tailored to their goals—whether navigating fertility treatments, preparing for birth, or building lifelong tools for wellness and self-care.

Meet the Heart Behind The Fertility Collective

Hi, I'm Jacqui Pilla

I'm a certified hypnotherapist, fertility coach, childbirth educator, and the founder of The Fertility Collective—a community created to support individuals and couples through fertility, pregnancy, birth, and the journey beyond.

My passion for this work began many years ago when my husband and I navigated our own fertility journey while building our family. That experience inspired me to learn everything I could about fertility, pregnancy, birth, and the powerful connection between the mind and body. What began as a personal journey became a calling to help others feel supported, informed, and empowered during some of life's most meaningful transitions.

After becoming a childbirth educator and doula, I continued my training in fertility-focused hypnosis and developed a private practice dedicated to helping individuals and couples find greater calm, confidence, and resilience. Today, I also work with growing families as a childbirth educator at Stork Ready.

I founded The Fertility Collective to be more than a place for guided hypnosis or coaching. I envisioned a welcoming community where people could find education, compassionate guidance, meaningful connection, and evidence-informed tools to nurture both emotional and physical well-being.

Whether you're trying to conceive naturally, navigating IUI or IVF, preparing for birth, or simply looking for someone to walk beside you during an important season of life, you'll be welcomed with understanding, encouragement, and personalized support.

It would be my privilege to walk alongside you as you navigate your own unique journey.
